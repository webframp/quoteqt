Get Started

Add a !quote command to your Twitch or YouTube stream in minutes!

Nightbot Setup

  1. Suggest quotes for your channel (or ask your viewers to)
  2. Add this command in Nightbot:
    !commands add !quote $(urlfetch https://quoteqt.exe.dev/api/quote)
  3. That's it! Type !quote in chat to get a random quote.
