About Moderators

Moderators can:

  • Sign in via Twitch (no exe.dev account needed)
  • View snapshot history for their assigned channels
  • View and download snapshots
  • Compare diffs between snapshots

Moderators cannot:

  • Create new snapshots
  • Restore snapshots to Nightbot
  • Delete snapshots
  • Diff against live Nightbot config
  • Access other admin features

Note: Channel owners automatically have moderator access to their own channels.
